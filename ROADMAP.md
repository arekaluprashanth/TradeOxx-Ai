# TradeOXX AI - Product Roadmap

This document outlines the strategic vision and feature pipeline for TradeOXX AI following the V1.0 Launch.

## Next Up: V1.1 (The "Data" Update)
*Target: Q1 2027*

**Focus:** Expanding market coverage and improving data fidelity.
- **Live Brokerage Integrations:** Plaid / Yodlee integration to automatically sync real portfolios instead of manual entry.
- **Crypto Expansion:** Full integration of Top 100 cryptocurrencies with real-time WebSocket data.
- **Advanced Charting:** Adding 50+ new technical indicators to the charting engine (Bollinger Bands, Ichimoku Clouds).
- **Mobile Web Optimization:** Heavy focus on making the complex charting interface usable on iOS/Android Safari.

## Later: V1.2 (The "Intelligence" Update)
*Target: Q2 2027*

**Focus:** Deepening the AI capabilities and personalization.
- **Custom AI Personas:** Users can switch the AI from "Educational Coach" to "Aggressive Quant Analyst."
- **Voice Interactivity:** Ability to ask the AI questions using voice on mobile devices.
- **Predictive Backtesting:** Ask the AI "What would have happened if I bought AAPL before earnings?" and generate an instant visual backtest.

## Future Vision: V2.0 (The "Enterprise" Update)
*Target: Q4 2027*

**Focus:** B2B scaling and team collaboration.
- **Team Workspaces:** Allow prop-trading desks or investment clubs to share a single unified dashboard and AI context.
- **API Platform:** Expose the TradeOXX Market & AI engines via REST API so developers can build apps on top of our intelligence layer.
- **White-labeling:** Allow financial advisors to brand the TradeOXX dashboard to share insights with their clients.
