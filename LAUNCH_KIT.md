## 3. Post-Launch Community Strategy

1. **Discord Launch**: Open the TradeOXX Community Discord. Create channels for `#feature-requests`, `#market-chat`, and `#bug-reports`.
2. **Support SLA**: For the first 30 days, guarantee a < 2 hour response time on all support emails to build early-adopter trust.
3. **Feedback Loop**: After a user has been active for 7 days, trigger an automated email asking for a 5-minute feedback call with the founders.
